# Awake — Privacy Policy

*Last updated: March 31, 2026*

## Overview

Awake ("the App") is a macOS menu bar utility developed by Modane Management. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data the App collects and how it is used.

## Data Collection

**Awake does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.**

Specifically:
- No analytics or tracking
- No crash reporting sent to us
- No user accounts or registration
- No data shared with third parties by us
- No cookies or web tracking

## API Keys

The App allows you to optionally configure API keys for third-party AI services (Anthropic, OpenAI, or Google AI). These keys are:
- Stored locally on your device in the Application Support directory
- Never transmitted to us or any server other than the AI provider you selected
- Used solely to process your natural language commands within the App
- Deletable at any time through the App's settings

## Third-Party AI Services

When you use the AI command feature, your command text is sent directly to the AI provider whose API key you configured. This communication is between your device and the AI provider. We do not intermediate, log, or store these communications. Please refer to the respective privacy policies of:
- Anthropic: https://www.anthropic.com/privacy
- OpenAI: https://openai.com/privacy
- Google: https://policies.google.com/privacy

## Local Data

The App stores the following data locally on your device:
- Your configuration preferences (watch list, rules, settings)
- AI API keys (in Application Support, protected by macOS file permissions)

This data never leaves your device.

## Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect data from children under 13.
